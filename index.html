📝 Class 10 Maths Top PYQs

Q1: Prove that √5 is an irrational number.Repeated 2019, 2022, 2024

Short Trick Strategy: Assume √5 = a/b (co-prime integers). Square both sides: 5b² = a². This implies 5 divides a, hence 5 divides b too. This contradicts our co-prime assumption. Therefore, √5 is irrational.

Q2: Find roots of quadratic equation: 2x² - 7x + 3 = 0.CBSE 2023

Solution: Splitting middle term: 2x² - 6x - x + 3 = 0 → 2x(x - 3) - 1(x - 3) = 0. Roots are x = 3 and x = 1/2.
📝 Class 10 Science Top PYQs

Q1: Why do stars twinkle but planets do not?Repeated 2018, 2020, 2023

Ans: Stars point-sized light source hote hain aur unki light dynamic air layers ki wajah se continuous layers track badalti hai (Atmospheric Refraction). Planets closer hain aur extended sources hote hain, isliye total variation change cancel out ho jata hai.

Q2: Write functions of Auxin and Abscisic Acid plant hormones.CBSE 2024

Ans: Auxin cell elongation aur shoot apex ki growth badhata hai. Abscisic Acid growth inhibitor hai jo seeds dormancy aur leaf falling control karta hai.

SECTION B (3-Marks Conceptual Problems)

Maths Mock Prob: A tangent PA from a point P to a circle with center O is inclined to the radius at an angle of 90°. If PA = 4cm, find diameter if OP = 5cm.

Using Pythagoras in ΔOAP: OA² + AP² = OP² → OA² + 4² = 5² → OA = 3cm (radius). So, Diameter = 2 × 3 = 6cm.
